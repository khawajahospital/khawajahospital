Comprehensive Healthcare Services

At Khawaja Hospital, we offer a wide range of medical services to cater to the diverse healthcare needs of our community. Our team of highly qualified doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals is committed to providing the best possible care using the latest medical technologies.

Emergency Care

Our emergency department is equipped to handle a variety of critical conditions with a dedicated team available 24/7. We prioritize quick response and quality care for life-threatening situations.

Outpatient Consultation

Our outpatient department offers specialized consultations across various medical fields, including general medicine, cardiology, orthopedics, and more, ensuring comprehensive care for non-emergency cases.

General Surgery

Our skilled surgeons perform a wide range of general surgeries, from minor procedures to major operations, ensuring the best possible outcomes with advanced surgical techniques.

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department specializes in caring for children, providing expert care from newborns to teenagers, focusing on prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of childhood diseases.

Radiology & Imaging

Our state-of-the-art imaging services include X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasounds, allowing for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning with the help of advanced radiological technology.

Laboratory Testing

We provide a wide range of laboratory tests, from routine blood work to specialized diagnostics, all performed by highly trained lab technicians to ensure fast and accurate results.

Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy offers a variety of medications and healthcare products, with trained pharmacists available to provide medication counseling and ensure the proper usage of prescriptions.

Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Our physiotherapy department helps patients recover from surgeries, injuries, and chronic conditions, providing customized rehabilitation plans and therapy sessions to improve mobility and quality of life.

Vaccinations

We offer a comprehensive vaccination program for both adults and children, ensuring protection against a variety of infectious diseases and helping to maintain public health safety.
